Privacy Policy – Sheet Protector Pro

Effective Date: October 30, 2025 | Location: Mumbai, India

Sheet Protector Pro is a Google Sheets™ add-on that helps users protect formulas, ranges, and entire sheets from accidental edits.

Data Access and Usage

The add-on requires access to the following data to function:

  • Google Sheets™ Data: The add-on requires access to your spreadsheets to apply and manage range/sheet protections, lock formulas, and generate audit logs.
  • User Settings: The add-on may store simple user preferences (such as last action for undo) using Google Apps Script’s built-in PropertiesService. This data remains within your Google account and is not transmitted externally.
  • User Email and Profile Information: The add-on requests access to your Google account’s email address and basic profile information solely to identify the active user and personalize the experience (e.g., "Only You" mode). This information is not stored or shared outside Google services.

All processing occurs within your Google account environment.

Data Sharing

The add-on does not collect, transmit, or share your data with any third-party services.

Security and Data Protection Mechanisms

We follow Google’s recommended practices for add-ons. Sensitive data (e.g., spreadsheet content, user email, and profile information) is protected using:

  • Google’s built-in encryption at rest (AES-256)
  • Encryption in transit (TLS 1.2+)
  • OAuth-scoped permissions with no persistent external storage
  • Regular code reviews and dependency updates

All interactions use Google’s secure APIs. Your data remains private and under your control.

Data Retention and Deletion

Sheet Protector Pro does not retain user data beyond the session required for functionality.

  • Spreadsheet content and protections are processed in-memory.
  • Settings (e.g., last action) are stored temporarily in Google Apps Script’s internal storage and cleared when the add-on is uninstalled or reset.
  • To delete data: Uninstall the add-on via Google Workspace Marketplace or clear settings manually in the sidebar.
  • Revoke access anytime in your Google Account under 'Third-party apps with account access'.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected here with a new effective date.
