Frequently Asked Questions

Does it collect my data?

No. All processing happens inside your Google Sheets. No data leaves your account.

Why does it need email access?

To identify you for "Only You" mode and personalize the experience. Not stored or shared.

Can I undo a protection?

Yes! Click Undo Last in the sidebar.

How do I uninstall?

Extensions → Add-ons → Manage add-ons → Remove
